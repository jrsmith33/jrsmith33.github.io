Science Fair Project: Effect of Music on Cognitive Performance

For my science fair project, I explored the effect of different music genres (Jazz, Pop, and Silence) on cognitive performance. I had participants take a math test while listening to different types of music, and the results showed that Pop music helped the most in completing the problems.
